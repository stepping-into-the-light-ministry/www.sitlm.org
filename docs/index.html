Stepping Into The Light, Inc. was founded on September 16th, 1994 to meet the needs of the growing number of men suffering from substance abuse.

Jerome Bracely, our founder and Executive Director, has led the organization through a period of sustained growth.

The organization started out doing inpatient prevention work with one endorsement and small contributions.
